Rules of Blackjack

Player attempts to beat the dealer by getting a count as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21.

Player starts with 100 points. Each turn's wager is 10 points.

Cards will be shuffled. Dealer deals two cards to each player.

If player or dealer draws ace and 10, jack, queen or king, they win the turn with Blackjack cards

Player can choose to hit (draw another card from the deck) or stand (proceed without adding another card).

After player is done with the turn, dealer makes their play.

Cards of the player and dealer are compared - the higher number wins.

Goodluck!

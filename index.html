Instructions:
1. There will be only two players. You and the computer.
2. The computer will always be the dealer.
3. Both of you will get two cards to start.
4. You go first, and decides if you want to hit (draw a card) or stand (end your turn).
5. The dealer has to hit if their hand is below 17. Each players' score is the total of their card ranks. Jacks/Queen/Kings are 10. Aces can be 1 or 11.
6. The player who is closer to, but not above 21 wins the hand.

How to Play

This is a Blackjack game for 1 player against the dealer, Pepe the Frog.
Players are given 100 coins at the start of the game and wagers a number of coins before each round starts.
The player loses once the coins are used up!

The rules for Blackjack are as follows:

  1. The player gets dealt two cards to start.
  2. The player goes first, and decides if they want to hit (draw a card) or stand (end their turn).
  3. The dealer has to hit if their hand is below 17.
  4. Each players' score is the total of their card ranks.
  5. Number cards count as their number, the jack, queen, and king count as 10, and aces count as either 1 or 11 according to the player's choice.
  6. The player who is closer to, but not above 21 wins the hand.
  7. A player gets "Blackjack" if the total score is 21 on the first two cards!
  8. The player wins immediately if he gets "Blackjack" unless dealer also has one, in which case the hand ties.

Welcome to Blackjack! Click on the GO! button to get started.

The objective of this game is to win by creating card totals higher than those of the dealer's hand but not exceeding 21, or by stopping at a total in the hope that dealer will bust.

A player total of 21 on the first two cards is a "blackjack," and the player wins immediately unless dealer also has one, in which case the hand ties.

After the dealer shuffles the deck, the player can choose to hit (take another card) or stand (end turn and stop without taking a card).

After the player is done with the turn, dealer makes their play.

Cards of the player and dealer are compared - the higher number wins.

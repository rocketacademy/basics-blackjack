Rules of the game.

1. There will be only two players. One human and one computer.

2. The computer will always be the dealer.

3. Each player gets dealt two cards to start.

4. The player goes first, and decides if they want to hit (draw a card) or stand (end their turn).

5. The dealer has to hit if their hand is below 17. -> Draw a Card

6. Whoever has the higher Score and lesser than 21 Win!

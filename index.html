Welcome to JR's blackjack game!

Press Deal to start the game. The hand closest to 21 points will win the game!

The player can either 'hit' or 'stand'. If the player calls out 'HIT', they are given an extra card. They can then either call out 'HIT' again, or 'STAND' if they do not wish to draw any more cards. The player can 'HIT' as many times as they wish, but have to aim not to bust (exceed a total of 21). More information about the game can be found below.

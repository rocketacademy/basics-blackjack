Rules of Blackjack

Player attempts to beat the dealer by getting a value as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21.

Player and dealer starts with 100 points.

Each round is worth 20 points and whoever loses all their points first forfeits the game!

Cards will be shuffled. Dealer deals two cards to each player.

If player or dealer draws ace and 10, jack, queen or king, they win the turn.

Player can choose to hit (draw another card from the deck) or stand (proceed without adding another card).

After player is done with the turn, dealer makes their play.

Cards of the player and dealer are compared - the higher number wins.

Good luck partner!

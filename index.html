BlackJack Game


♡ ♢ ♤ ♧ How to play BlackJack❓ ♡ ♢ ♤ ♧

1. Player and dealer gets 2 cards each.
2. If player or dealer gets 21 on first draw. It's BLACKJACK!
3. If player or dealer get 21 from second draw onwards its 21.
4. If player or dealer draws more than 21 its a bust.
5. Closest hand to 21 wins.

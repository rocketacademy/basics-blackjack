Hello there, fancy a game of Blackjack?
Hit "Deal" to get started.


House Rules

Objective of the Game
The player attempts to beat the dealer by getting a count as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21.

Flow of the Game
The deck is shuffled and cards are dealt alternately between the player and dealer until both has 2 cards each. If either participant has no instant-win conditions, the player is free to draw cards until he decides to stand. The dealer will draw if required after the player stands, and results will be revealed.

Instant Win Conditions
Blackjack: Your first two cards are an Ace and any card with a value of 10 (10, Jack, Queen, King).
Two Aces: Your first two cards are a pair of Aces.
5 Cards: You have 5 cards that have a total equal to or less than 21.

House Rules
Minimum Limit: Participants have to draw a card if their total card value is 15 or less.
Variable Ace: Aces count as 11 if you have 2 cards, and 1 or 10 if more than 2 cards.

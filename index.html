LET'S PLAY:

~BLACKJACK~



Welcome to the game of Blackjack!

Here are the rules:

Your goal is to get as close to 21 points without going over.
Cards 2 - 10 will have the same points as their cards.
Jack, Queen, King are worth 10 points each.
Aces are special! They are worth 1, 10, or 11 points.
Here are some special win conditions!

Special Scenarios

10/Jack/Queen/King/Ace + Ace = Blackjack! You immediately win the round if this is your opening hand!
